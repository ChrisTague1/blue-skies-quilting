Find Inspiration at Blue Sky Quilt Shop

Welcome to Blue Sky Quilt Shop, your premier destination for high-quality quilting fabrics and endless creative possibilities. Nestled in the charming downtown of LeClaire, Iowa, we invite you to explore our ever-growing collection of fabric and discover new inspiration. Whether you're a seasoned quilter or just starting your journey, our friendly staff and bountiful samples of inspirational project ideas and kits will ignite your creativity and foster a sense of community. Shop at Blue Sky Quilt Shop, a place of friendship and fabric!
